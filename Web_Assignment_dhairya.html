  1. What type of Movies I like . . .

    2. Sci-fi—well, to be more accurate, sci-fi with a thoughtful, creative premise. Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind, Inception, and 2001: A Space Odyssey are the kind of sci-fi movies I enjoy. Although, everyone here knows I also enjoy Star Wars :) However, I also enjoyed Guardians of the Galaxy, Valerian, Star Trek: Beyond—oh, screw a “thoughtful premise”. Who needs that anyway? I’d take a lightsaber over a profound theme any day.

    Animated movies- Animated films just have so much more thought and work put into them than live-action ones do. Not to mention the fact that there is an entire artistic element involved as well, which often takes years to execute. I especially love Studio Ghibli, with Miyazaki and Takahata, but also Tomm Moore and pretty much anything from GKIDS. I also love stop-motion a lot, including Fantastic Mr. Fox, Nightmare Before Christmas, and My Life as a Zucchini
